About ShaadiHall

Vijayapura's dedicated marriage hall and wedding vendor discovery platform

Our Story

Built for Vijayapura Weddings

ShaadiHall was founded with a simple mission: to make wedding planning in Vijayapura, Karnataka easier, more transparent and more enjoyable for every family.

We know how important your wedding day is. Finding the right hall, comparing prices, understanding amenities, checking availability — all of this used to require visiting multiple venues in person. We built ShaadiHall to bring all of this information to your fingertips.

From our roots in Vijayapura, we plan to expand to Bagalkot, Bidar, Dharwad and eventually across Karnataka — always staying focused on making local wedding planning effortless.
